## Use Case : Financial Service Industry

Genesist revolutionizes the financial services sector by focusing on Core Banking System (CBS) Analysis. This includes tasks such as analyzing and automating Rencana Business Bank (RBB) or bank business plans, monitoring Non-Performing Loans (NPL), and assessing the CAMEL framework. Additionally, Genesist enhances operations across front office, middle office, and back office functions.
