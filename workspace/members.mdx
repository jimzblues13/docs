## How to create workspace & invite members

- **Navigate to the Bottom-Left Corner:** Click on the Manage Workspace icon.


- **Create a New Workspace:** Click the **+New** button to create a new workspace.


- **Customize Your Workspace:** Input your workspace name and choose a symbol or upload your own.
