## Monitoring Workspace Usage
Learn how your workspace utilizes resources and ensure your team operates within the limits of your chosen plan.

- **View Usage Details**, Navigate to Usage to see an overview of your current usage, including data storage, compute hours, and other resources.
- **Understand Your Limits**, Review the limits associated with your current plan.  You can find the details about your plan's limits by checking your package information.
