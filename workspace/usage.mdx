## Monitoring Workspace Usage

Understand how your workspace consumes resources and ensure your team operates within the limits of your selected plan.

- **View Usage Details**, Navigate to Usage to see an overview of your current usage, including data storage, compute hours, and other resources.
- **Understand Your Limits**, Review the limits associated with your current plan. You can find detailed information about plan limits on your package.
