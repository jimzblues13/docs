title: 'How can I get my credentials?'
---
Learn how to get your <code>Company ID</code> and <code>private-key</code>.

## Authentication

Retrieve your <code>Company ID</code> and <code>private-key</code> by:

1. Logging into Genesist Console [Bossman](https://backoffice-genesist.soca.ai/sign-in).
2. Click **API Keys** menu.
3. Navigating to the <code>Keys section</code>.
4. Copying your <code>Company ID</code> and <code>private-key</code>.
