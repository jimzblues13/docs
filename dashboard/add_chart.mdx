How to add a chart to dashboard, follow these steps:
- Open Dashboard, On the right side click **Edit** button. You’ll see the **Add Chart** option appear on the left side.
- **Add Chart**, Choose the chart you’ve created or use the updated chart features for enhanced visualization options.
